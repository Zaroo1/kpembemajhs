About Kpembe M/A JHS

Kpembe M/A Junior High School, nestled in the quiet town of Kpembe in Salaga, Savannah Region, has been a beacon of knowledge and discipline for decades. We believe in empowering our students through quality education and character building.

Our Vision

To become a center of academic excellence in the Savannah Region, producing responsible, disciplined, and capable young leaders who will shape the future of Ghana.

Our Mission

To provide quality education through dedicated teaching, moral training, and a supportive learning environment that nurtures both academic and personal growth.

Message from the Headteacher

As the Headteacher of this great school, I am proud of our students' achievements and the dedication of our teachers. We continue to grow and innovate, and I encourage our alumni, parents, and community members to support us in building a brighter future for our children.

— Mr. Kwame Ansah, Headteacher
