Academic Life

Kpembe M/A JHS offers a well-rounded curriculum based on the Ghana Education Service (GES) standards, focusing on both intellectual and moral development.

Subjects Offered

English Language

Developing reading, writing, speaking and listening skills for effective communication.

Mathematics

Building foundational math skills in algebra, geometry, and problem-solving techniques.

Integrated Science

Exploring biology, chemistry, physics and earth sciences through practical experiments.

Social Studies

Understanding Ghanaian history, geography, government and civic responsibilities.

ICT

Developing computer literacy, digital skills and responsible technology use.

Religious & Moral Education

Exploring religious diversity, ethical principles and moral development.

Career Technology

Practical skills development in technical and vocational areas.

Creative Arts & Design

Exploring visual arts, music, drama and developing creative expression.

Ghanaian Language (Gonja)

Preserving cultural heritage through Gonja language and traditions.
